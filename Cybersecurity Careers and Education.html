Cybersecurity Education & Career Development
Site Author United States government: Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency
Abstract The website is about promoting cybersecurity education and career development, with a special focus on initiatives led by the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) in the United States. Its core mission is to build a strong cybersecurity workforce to enhance national security. They're doing this through a range of approaches, including bringing cybersecurity concepts into classrooms from an early age with programs like the Cybersecurity Education and Training Assistance Program (CETAP) and providing training to teachers. There's also a strong emphasis on diversity and inclusion, with programs targeting underserved communities. CISA collaborates with educational institutions and the private sector to provide cybersecurity monitoring and training, ensuring that even smaller organizations have access to the cybersecurity skills they need. They work closely with colleges and universities to designate them as National Centers of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense and encourage students to pursue cybersecurity degrees through scholarship programs. They provide a framework for categorizing cybersecurity work with the National Initiative for Cybersecurity Education (NICE Framework), and their National Initiative for Cybersecurity Careers and Studies (NICCS) website offers a wealth of information about education, training, and career opportunities in the field. In a nutshell, this website serves as a human-friendly resource for anyone interested in a career in cybersecurity, offering guidance, support, and opportunities to help build a skilled and diverse cybersecurity workforce.

GUIDE TO CYBER SECURITY CAREERS: IS THIS THE PATH FOR YOU?
Site Author Cybersecurity Education
Abstract This website serves as a comprehensive guide for individuals considering a career in cybersecurity. It helps you navigate the complex world of cybersecurity by providing information on various career paths, educational requirements, certifications, and estimated salaries in the field. It emphasizes the importance of cybersecurity professionals, especially in today's digital age, where safeguarding online data is crucial. The website discusses different roles within cybersecurity, from entry-level positions to specialized roles like incident responders, security administrators, and ethical hackers. It also provides insights into the skills and traits that can be valuable in these roles. Additionally, the site offers guidance on educational paths, job responsibilities, salary expectations, and potential career growth in cybersecurity. Whether you're just starting or looking to advance your career in cybersecurity, this website aims to provide the information you need to make informed decisions and succeed in the field.
